## Overview

SoGaffer is a complementary tool — you use it alongside your Sorare account to make better lineup decisions each gameweek. This guide walks you through the full process from account setup to submitting your first optimized lineup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Sorare account">
    If you already have a Sorare account, skip to the next step.

    1. Go to [sorare.com](https://sorare.com) and click **Sign up**.
    2. Enter your email address and choose a password.
    3. Verify your email and complete your profile.

    <Note>
      Sorare requires identity verification before you can buy or trade cards. You can still receive free Common cards and explore competitions while verification is pending.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up SoGaffer">
    Clone the SoGaffer repository from GitHub:

    ```bash
    git clone https://github.com/iglesiash/sogaffer.git
    cd sogaffer
    ```

    Follow any setup instructions in the repository's README to install dependencies and configure the tool for your environment.

    <Tip>
      Check the repository's README and any open issues on [GitHub](https://github.com/iglesiash/sogaffer) for the most up-to-date setup instructions as the project evolves.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Gather your card collection information">
    Before running SoGaffer, know which cards you have available to play this gameweek:

    - Log in to [sorare.com](https://sorare.com) and open your **My Cards** section.
    - Note the players, positions, and rarities of the cards you are willing to enter into a competition.
    - Identify which cards are already locked into another competition and unavailable.

    <Note>
      SoGaffer works with the card information you provide. Having an accurate view of your available collection before you start will produce better recommendations.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Input your cards and run the optimizer">
    With SoGaffer running, input your available player cards. The optimizer analyzes:

    - Each player's recent form and score history
    - Upcoming fixture difficulty for the current gameweek
    - Whether any players have blank or double gameweeks
    - The rarity bonus multiplier applied to each card

    SoGaffer then recommends the strongest five-player lineup from your available options.

    <Tip>
      Run the optimizer at least 24–48 hours before the gameweek deadline so you have time to factor in late team news and injury updates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the recommendation">
    SoGaffer's recommendation is a starting point, not a final answer. Before accepting it:

    - Check that no recommended player has a blank gameweek (SoGaffer flags these automatically)
    - Review the projected scores and the reasoning behind each selection
    - Check for late injury news or rotation risk for any selected player
    - Consider whether the competition you are entering calls for a safe (consistent) or ceiling (high-upside) lineup

    See [Lineup optimization](/concepts/lineup-optimization) for a full explanation of how SoGaffer weighs these factors.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your lineup on Sorare">
    Once you are satisfied with your five-player selection, submit it directly on Sorare:

    1. Log in to [sorare.com](https://sorare.com) and open the **Play** tab.
    2. Select the competition you are entering.
    3. Add the five players SoGaffer recommended, matching each to the correct positional slot.
    4. Review your lineup, then click **Submit** before the gameweek deadline.

    <Warning>
      SoGaffer does not submit lineups to Sorare on your behalf. You must enter your selections manually on the Sorare platform before the competition deadline.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>
