## What is SoGaffer?

SoGaffer — **S**orare **O**ptimal **G**ameweek **A**lignment **F**ormation **F**or **E**ffective **R**esults — is a complementary tool designed for Sorare fantasy football managers. It helps you cut through the noise each gameweek and identify the strongest possible lineup from your card collection.

## Why use SoGaffer?

Sorare rewards managers who consistently make better lineup decisions than the competition. SoGaffer makes those decisions faster and more data-driven by combining player form, fixture difficulty, card rarity bonuses, and blank/double gameweek detection into a single recommendation.
