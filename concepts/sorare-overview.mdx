Sorare is a global fantasy football game built on digital player cards. You collect cards representing real-world footballers, assemble a team each gameweek, and earn points based on how those players perform in actual matches.

SoGaffer sits on top of that process and helps you pick the best possible lineup from the cards you own.

## How Sorare works

Each gameweek, you enter a competition by fielding a team of five players:

- 1 goalkeeper
- 1 defender
- 1 midfielder
- 1 forward
- 1 extra player (any outfield position)

Players score points based on their real match performance — goals, assists, clean sheets, saves, and more. Your total team score is the sum of each player's score, adjusted by their card rarity.

<Info>
  Every player in your lineup must have a match in the gameweek. A player with no fixture scores zero and wastes a slot in your team.
</Info>

## Card rarities and score multipliers

Every Sorare card has a rarity tier. Higher rarity cards apply a score multiplier, rewarding you for owning premium cards.

## How competitions work

Sorare runs competitions across multiple tiers and leagues. Each competition specifies:

- **Which card rarities are eligible** — some competitions accept only Rare or above
- **The scoring period** — typically a single gameweek
- **Prize structure** — card rewards, ETH, or both

You submit your five-player lineup before the gameweek deadline. Once matches kick off, scores are calculated in real time.

## Why lineup optimization matters

With hundreds of cards in your collection and dozens of gameweeks per season, picking the right five players each week is the core skill in Sorare. The decisions compound:

- A player in a blank gameweek scores zero
- A double-gameweek player has two chances to score big
- Playing a higher-rarity card on a player with a tough fixture wastes your multiplier advantage
- Some competitions reward consistent, safe scores; others reward high-ceiling picks

Making these calls manually — tracking fixtures, form, and rarity bonuses across your whole collection — is time-consuming and error-prone.

## Where SoGaffer adds value

SoGaffer automates the analytical work so you can focus on the final decision. It:

- Identifies which of your players have fixtures this gameweek (and which are blank)
- Flags double-gameweek opportunities
- Weighs player form against fixture difficulty

- Suggests lineups optimized for either consistency or ceiling, depending on your goal
