<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is SoGaffer?">
    SoGaffer (Sorare Optimal Gameweek Alignment Formation For Effective Results) is a lineup optimization tool designed to help Sorare players build stronger fantasy football teams each gameweek. It analyzes your available player cards, upcoming fixture difficulty, player form, and rarity bonuses to recommend the best possible five-player lineup for your collection.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is SoGaffer free to use?">
    Yes, SoGaffer is free to use. There are no subscription fees or premium tiers. The project is open source and community-driven, so you can use all features at no cost.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SoGaffer guarantee wins?">
    No. SoGaffer provides data-driven recommendations based on form, fixture difficulty, and projected scores, but football is inherently unpredictable. A player with an excellent projection can have an off match, and a surprise result can flip any gameweek. SoGaffer improves your decision-making process over time — it does not guarantee any individual result.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which Sorare leagues does SoGaffer support?">
    SoGaffer is designed to support all major Sorare competition formats, including the Champion, Challenger, and Contender leagues as well as rarity-specific leagues (Rare, Super Rare, and Unique). Coverage of specific football leagues depends on the fixture data available. Check the **Fixtures** tab in SoGaffer to see which leagues are active for a given gameweek.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often is player data updated?">
    Player scores and fixture data are updated each gameweek as results come in. Projections and form indicators refresh automatically based on the latest available match data. For the most accurate injury and availability information, we recommend cross-referencing with official club and league sources, as team news can change rapidly in the 24 hours before kick-off.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SoGaffer work for all football leagues on Sorare?">
    SoGaffer aims to cover all football leagues available on Sorare, but data quality and fixture availability may vary by league. Major European leagues (Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1) have the most comprehensive coverage. Smaller or less-documented leagues may have limited projection data. If you notice a league is missing or incomplete, consider raising a feature request on GitHub.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I override SoGaffer's recommendations?">
    Yes, absolutely. SoGaffer's recommendations are a starting point, not a mandate. You can manually select any player from your card collection for any slot, regardless of what the optimizer suggests. Your knowledge of a specific team's form, a manager's tendencies, or upcoming transfer windows is valuable context that SoGaffer may not fully capture.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is SoGaffer affiliated with Sorare?">
    No. SoGaffer is an independent, community-built tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Sorare in any way. Sorare is a registered trademark of Sorare SAS. SoGaffer is a third-party complement to the Sorare platform.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug or request a feature?">
    You can report bugs and request features directly on the SoGaffer GitHub repository. Open an issue with a clear description of the problem or the feature you would like to see. The more detail you provide — including your browser, gameweek, and steps to reproduce — the faster an issue can be investigated and resolved.
  </Accordion>
