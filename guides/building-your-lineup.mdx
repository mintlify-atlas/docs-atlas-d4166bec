---
title: Building your lineup
description: A step-by-step guide to building an optimized Sorare lineup using SoGaffer.
---

SoGaffer walks you through the full lineup-building process — from inputting your cards to reviewing your final five before submitting to Sorare. Follow the steps below to get the most out of every gameweek.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a new lineup build session">
    Open SoGaffer and click **New lineup**. Select the gameweek you are building for from the dropdown. SoGaffer loads the latest fixture data, player projections, and rarity information for that gameweek automatically.

    <Tip>
      Start your session at least 48 hours before the gameweek deadline. This gives you time to factor in late team news and injury updates.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Input your available cards">
    In the **My cards** panel, add the player cards you have available to play. You can:

    - Search by player name
    - Filter by position (GK, DEF, MID, FWD)
    - Filter by rarity (Common, Limited, Rare, Super Rare, Unique)

    SoGaffer matches each card to live player data and applies the correct rarity bonus multiplier. Make sure every card you are willing to play is added before reviewing recommendations.

    <Note>
      SoGaffer does not automatically sync with your Sorare account. You must input your cards manually each session.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review player projections and recommendations">
    Once your cards are loaded, navigate to the **Recommendations** tab. SoGaffer ranks your available players for each position based on:

    - Recent form and score history
    - Fixture difficulty for the upcoming gameweek
    - Double gameweek opportunities (players with two fixtures)
    - Rarity bonus applied to the projected score

    Click any player row to see a detailed breakdown of their projection. See [Analyzing players](/guides/analyzing-players) for a deeper look at how to interpret these metrics.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your final five">
    Build your team by selecting one player for each slot:

    | Slot | Position | Notes |
    |---|---|---|
    | Goalkeeper | GK | Clean sheet bonus is significant — prioritize good defensive fixtures |
    | Defender | DEF | Fixture difficulty matters more here than raw form |
    | Midfielder | MID | Look for high-volume players with assist and goal potential |
    | Forward | FWD | Prioritize attackers with favorable fixtures and recent scoring form |
    | Extra | Any | Use this slot for your best double-gameweek player or highest-ceiling card |

    SoGaffer highlights conflicting choices — for example, if two of your selections face each other — so you can make an informed call.

    <Tip>
      Do not automatically default to your highest-rarity card for every slot. A Common card with a great fixture can outscore a Rare card facing a top-four defence. See [Gameweek strategy](/guides/gameweek-strategy) for more on balancing rarity vs. form.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your lineup">
    Before you exit SoGaffer, use the **Lineup summary** panel to review:

    - Projected total score and score range
    - Rarity bonuses applied per card
    - Any players with injury flags or uncertain availability
    - Coverage of blank vs. double gameweeks across your five slots

    If SoGaffer surfaces a warning (yellow or red indicator), click it to read the specific concern before proceeding.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit to Sorare">
    SoGaffer does not submit lineups directly to Sorare. Once you are satisfied with your selection, open your Sorare account and enter the same five players in the relevant competition. Double-check that each card you selected in SoGaffer is the one you submit on Sorare — rarity and card edition affect your bonus multiplier.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common mistakes to avoid

<Warning>
  **Playing a card into a blank gameweek.** If a player has no fixture this gameweek, they score zero. Always confirm each player has a live fixture before submitting. SoGaffer flags blanks with a grey indicator in the player list.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Ignoring the extra slot.** Many players treat the extra slot as an afterthought. This slot often has the highest upside — use it for a player with a double gameweek or a standout fixture, not just your next-best remaining card.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Locking in your lineup too early.** Team news (injuries, suspensions, rotation) often breaks 24 hours before kick-off. Finalise your lineup as close to the deadline as practical.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Assuming rarity always wins.** A Unique card facing a strong opponent in poor form can underperform a well-placed Common card. Let the projections guide you, not rarity alone.
</Warning>
