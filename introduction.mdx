## What is SoGaffer?

SoGaffer — **S**orare **O**ptimal **G**ameweek **A**lignment **F**ormation **F**or **E**ffective **R**esults — is a complementary tool designed for competitive Sorare fantasy football managers. It takes the guesswork out of lineup building by helping you identify the strongest possible team for each gameweek.

Whether you're new to Sorare or a seasoned manager, SoGaffer gives you the data-driven insights you need to compete at the highest level.

## Why use SoGaffer?

Sorare is a global fantasy football game where you manage a squad of digital player cards. Building the optimal lineup requires balancing player form, fixture difficulty, card rarity bonuses, and positional constraints — all within a single gameweek window. SoGaffer makes that process faster and smarter.

## How it works

SoGaffer works alongside your existing Sorare account. It reads gameweek data and your available player cards, then applies optimization logic to recommend the best possible lineup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect to Sorare">
    Link SoGaffer to your Sorare account so it can access your card collection and gameweek data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your cards">
    SoGaffer inventories your player cards, including their rarity bonuses and current form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your optimal lineup">
    Receive a recommended formation and starting eleven based on projected scores and fixture analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your lineup">
    Review the recommendation, make any final adjustments, and submit on Sorare.
  </Step>
</Steps>
